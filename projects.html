Manage Your PDFs and Citations: Zotero & Mendeley

April 21, 2017, with Jennie Murack.

May 4, 2017, online workshop with with Stacey Snyder.

MIT Learn With the Libraries instruction session.

Using citation management software to create and maintain a collection of references or PDFs is common and important in today’s academic world. These tools will help you to save citations from your favorite databases and websites, store related PDFs or attachments, and quickly build a bibliography for your papers and publications. We’ll review Zotero and Mendeley and show how to use them together to help your manage your PDFs and citations.

Getting to Know You: Learning How to Learn About Each Other

MIT ICEO Community Discussion October 27, 2016

Working or studying in a diverse environment like MIT’s presents each of us with dozens of opportunities to learn from and about each other; it also can create unexpected frictions and pressures. Where’s the invisible line between asking a simple question and prying? How can we invite our colleagues and friends to tell us about their worlds without expecting them to bear the burden of teaching us? This dialog will give participants an opportunity to share their experiences of invitations to share – both the ones that felt good and the ones that might have gone better.
